Platform features

Everything your team needs

One CLI. Full visibility. No more lost env files or untracked secrets.

End-to-End Encryption

Every backup is encrypted before it leaves your machine. Your keys, your control — we never see the plaintext.

Version History

Every push creates a new versioned snapshot. Roll back to any previous state in seconds, with a full audit log.

Team Collaboration

Share env snapshots across your team with fine-grained permissions. No more Slack DMs of sensitive keys.

Drift Detection

envii watches for env changes in real time and alerts your team when production drifts from the last known-good backup.

CLI-First Interface

A single binary that fits into any workflow. Works with GitHub Actions, Docker, and your existing shell scripts out of the box.

Compliance Ready

Every operation is immutably logged. Meet SOC 2, ISO 27001, and internal audit requirements without extra tooling.
